# 🗿 Concrete Vault Stability Analyst (V2.0)

An institutional-grade risk assessment tool developed for the @ConcreteXYZ ecosystem. This application bridges the gap between complex DeFi volatility and capital security.

## ✨ Key Features
- **Volatility-Adjusted Yield Modeling:** Dynamically calculates the "Real Yield" by subtracting the risk premium of market volatility.
- **Stability Ratio (LaTeX Integrated):** Uses professional financial formulas to quantify the safety of your principal.
- **Institutional Comparison:** Real-time metrics comparing Standard DeFi risk vs. Concrete’s automated protection.
- **Interactive Technical FAQs:** A built-in knowledge base for community education.

## 🏛️ Why Use This Tool?
In traditional DeFi, high APY often masks high risk. This tool allows users to:
1. **Visualize the Floor:** Understand exactly how much of their principal is shielded.
2. **Calculate Efficiency:** Move beyond "Gross APY" to "Risk-Adjusted Gains."
3. **Transparent Logic:** Open-source formulas that align with the Moai philosophy of verifiable work.
